Example 5: geographic

Why

This example illustrates two basic approaches to visualizing data geographically. Choropleth maintains geographic shape, and uses color fill to show underlying data, while cartogram does away with the geographic shape.

A few design and technical notes

  • One of the data challenges is that indicator data and geographic information are in two different datasets. We must combine them first.
  • What is a choropleth effective for showing? What about a cartogram?
